---
title: "Eudaemona"
date: 2019-12-24T17:33:00-04:00
tags:
  - "poem"
---

Eudaemonia, eudaemonia,
And lack thereof.
Always listening, never learning,
Leonis esse, simulare.
I weep for you.
Fletus, simulatus,
Holy (wholly) eudaemonia.
Temporis morsus amarum,
Like moments,
Are lost.
Sonare, sonare,
And lack thereof.
Finally, sleep.
