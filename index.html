About
Sam Bossley, age 16, is the creator and developer of multiple websites for various organizations and clubs. His passion for web design started at the age of 13 and since then has coded over 25 websites. In his free time, he enjoys marching in the Floyd Central Marching Band, rocking out to music, and is genuinely interested in hire for a career in graphic design, physics, or engineering in his near future.
